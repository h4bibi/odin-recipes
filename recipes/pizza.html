Pizza Recipe

Description

Create your very own homemade pizza!

There are roughly 3 steps in making a great pizza: the dough, the sauce, and the toppings.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 1 packet of dry active yeast
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 2.5 cups of warm water
  • 7 cups flour
  • 1.5 teaspoon salt
  • 6 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil

Sauce

  • Largest can of tomato paste
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Oregano
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Water
  • Basil
  • Garlic powder
  • Onion powder
  • Sugar

Steps

Dough

  1. Pour sugar into 2.5 cups of warm water
  2. Pour packet of dry yeast into the same water and stir contents
  3. Wait for yeast to activate (15 minutes max), if yeast does not activate throw out entire mixture and start over.
  4. In a separate bowl, add 7 cups flour and 1.5 teaspoon of salt, mix.
  5. Once yeast mixture is activated, add to flour mixture alongside 6 tablespoons olive oil.
  6. Mix together by hand until it forms one big blob then kneed for 10 minutes minimum.

Kneeding

  1. On your workspace, throw enough flour to cover the area to begin kneeding
  2. To kneed, fold dough over itself and roll forwards and backwards in a left/right diagonal fashion.
  3. Apply flour over workspace and dough as needed
  4. Continue for a minimum of 10 minutes or until dough springs back when lightly pressed with one finger.
  5. Form dough into a ball; Lightly oil a bowl and place dough inside, lightly oil the dough's surface and cover the bowl with wrap.
  6. Let dough rise at room temperature or higher for at least 1 hour, overnight, or 24 hours.

After resting

  1. Uncover dough, flour and kneed for 1-2 minutes.
  2. Shape dough into a ball and flour surface.
  3. Let rise for another hour, prep sauce, toppings, and oven in the meantime.

Sauce

  1. Heat saucepan over medium heat; Once hot, add can of tomato paste.
  2. Gradually add water in small amount to dilute thick paste.
  3. Add garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, cayenne pepper (optional), basil, salt and pepper.
  4. Mix and let simmer 30 minutes to and hour.
  5. Taste and adjust salt, pepper, and cayenne to preference. Note: if slightly bitter, add sugar.

Final Steps

  1. Preheat oven to highest temperature
  2. Portion dough into 4 for 4 small/medium pizzas or into 2 for 2 large pizzas.
  3. Stretch dough and sprinkle Semonila flour or flour into pizza pan.
  4. Place the dough into the pan and generously add your sauce. Sprinkle cheese of your choice and decorate with your toppings.
  5. Insert pizza into the oven and remove once cheese is golden brown.
  6. If needed, sprinkle more shredded cheese onto pizza while in the oven.
  7. Remove, let sit, then cut into your delicious homemade pizza!
