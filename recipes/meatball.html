Meatball Recipe

Description

Easy Homemade meatball recipe: Ground beef, pork, or both!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of your favorite breadcrumbs
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh herbs: parsley/basil
  • 1/4 cup grated yellow onion
  • 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon fine sea salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. Make the panade. In a large mixing bowl, stir together the breadcrumbs and milk until combined. Set mixture aside to soak for 5-10 minutes.
  2. Combine remaining ingredients. Add the ground beef/pork/both, garlic, eggs, Parmesan, fresh herbs, onion, and Worcestershire sauce to the same mixing bowl. Sprinkle the oregano, salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes evenly on top. Use your hands to mix the ingredients until evely combined.
  3. Prep oven and baking sheet. Heat the oven to 425F and line a large baking sheet with parchment paper.
  4. Form the meatballs. Use a spoon, scoop, or good old fashioned hands to roll the mixture into balls and place them on the prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake. Bake for 10-15 minutes or until the internal temperature reads 160F. Remove pan and and transfer to a wire cooling rack.
  6. Serve. Serve meatballs and enjoy!
